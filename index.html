My Story

From the vineyards of France to the digital frontiers of today, my journey has been one of constant evolution and authentic connection. As a sommelier, I've learned that every wine tells a story. As an entrepreneur, I've discovered that every business should serve a purpose. As a podcaster, I've found that every conversation has the power to transform.

Today, I bridge these worlds through my various projects, each one designed to bring people together, foster growth, and create meaningful experiences. Whether it's through a carefully curated wine selection, an innovative tech platform, or a thought-provoking podcast episode, my mission remains the same: to help people unlock their potential and live more authentically.
