Who The F*** Is Paul-Henri

The story of a multi-faceted entrepreneur bridging wine, technology, and human potential

My Journey

Paul-Henri Cuvelier is a multifaceted entrepreneur who bridges the worlds of wine, technology, and human potential. Born with an insatiable curiosity and a passion for authentic connections, Paul-Henri has carved out a unique niche at the intersection of tradition and innovation.

From the vineyards of France to the digital frontiers of today, his journey has been one of constant evolution, always seeking to understand the deeper connections between craftsmanship, technology, and human experience.

The Sommelier

My journey with wine began not as a career choice, but as a calling. The complexity, the stories, the human connections forged over a shared bottle - wine became my first language for understanding the art of living well. As a certified sommelier, I've dedicated years to understanding not just the technical aspects of wine, but the emotions and experiences it represents.

The Entrepreneur

Entrepreneurship came naturally as I sought to create platforms and experiences that bring people together. From hospitality ventures to technology platforms, each project has been driven by a desire to solve real problems and create genuine value in people's lives.
